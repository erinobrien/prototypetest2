Trail Description

You can’t go wrong with a run on the trails of Mount Victoria. With easy access and a great trail network, this is one of the best road-to-trail options within 5 mins from the busy Wellington CBD. Mount Victoria or Mt Vic, as it is known to many of local runners, is simply not to be missed.

Trail Type

Sidewalk | Signposted Walkway | Public Roads

Trail Condition

Excellent - Can get slippery when wet

Fitness Level

Moderate - primarily uphill

Best Time to Run

Morning is the quietest time, however on a clear day sunset can be worth the crowds to view the city at this time

What You'll See

360 degree views of Wellington and on a clear day you might glimpse the top of the South Island. You’ll also see plenty of tourists!
