Trail Description

The Stellin Loop is located just off Tinakori Rd, in the heart of Wellington's Town Belt. While it is very close to the CBD, it will feel miles away from the bustling city. Once you reach the Te Ahumairangi Hill Lookout, you will be able to see 360 views of Wellington.

Trail Type

Bush Tracks | Signposted Walkway | Public Roads

Trail Condition

Great - Some sections are less maintained than others. Be careful of tree roots and logs blocking the trail

Fitness Level

High - primarily uphill and difficult terrain at times

Best Time to Run

This trail is usually quiet at all times, however avoid running after dark

What You'll See

Great views of Mt Victoria, Wellington CBD and Karori.
